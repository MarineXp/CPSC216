Upcoming Features

Solar-Powered Battery Packs

Development of a compact solar panel option to maintain full battery charge for the outdoor node, improving sustainability and lowering maintenance needs.

In Development

Next-Gen Solenoid Valve

Integration of a more robust solenoid valve that operates efficiently under low power and low water pressure conditions, increasing system reliability.

In Development

Instant Smart Watering UI

Upload a garden layout photo and place valves visually within the app. Tap set points on the image to instantly water specific zones without waiting for the automation cycle.

Planned

Soil Fertility Monitoring

Potential addition of soil nutrient sensors to monitor nutrient levels and automatically adjust watering cycles or send alerts when attention is needed. Additionally, a possible automatic nutrient replenishment system could be developed.

Concept

Weather Integration

Smart weather API integration allowing the system to predict rainfall and automatically delay watering schedules based on upcoming weather forecasts.
